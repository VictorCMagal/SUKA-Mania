## Why SukaMania Exists?

Every year, thousands of electronic devices are discarded incorrectly, causing environmental contamination and wasting valuable recyclable materials.

Many people want to recycle their electronics but face common obstacles:

* Lack of information about disposal locations.
* Limited awareness of environmental impacts.
* No incentives to encourage sustainable behavior.
* Difficulty finding reliable recycling programs.

**SukaMania** was created to bridge this gap by connecting citizens, recycling initiatives, and environmental education through a gamified platform.

---

## 💡 The Solution

SukaMania is a digital platform that encourages responsible electronic waste disposal through rewards, sustainability education, and user engagement.

Instead of simply informing users where to recycle, the platform motivates participation through a points system and interactive features that make environmental responsibility more accessible and enjoyable.

---

##  Core Experience

### Earn Points

Users receive points whenever they correctly dispose of electronic waste through registered collection initiatives.

### Redeem Rewards

Accumulated points can be exchanged for benefits and rewards available within the platform.

### Learn & Engage

Educational content helps users understand:

* Electronic waste impacts.
* Sustainability practices.
* Circular economy concepts.
* Responsible consumption habits.

### Track Your Impact

Users can visualize their contribution toward reducing environmental pollution and promoting recycling.
